About me

Hi, my name is Crystal Sills and I am a Software Engineer with a keen eye for design. My previous professional experience, as a Forensic Scientist, has provided me with a mild case of perfectionism and a methodical approach to completing tasks. After fourteen years in the field of forensics, I found myself looking to expand my skill set and discovered software engineering and web development. The challenge to produce clean code alongside excellent design provides a perfect intersection of my natural abilities in science and technology with my love of design and all things creative.

Featured Project

A SPACE TO CREATE AND RE-CREATE.

Remix culture, sometimes read-write culture, is a society that allows and encourages derivative works by combining or editing existing materials to produce a new creative work or product.

Remix is a creative platform for digital artists that encourages derivative works by combining or editing existing gallery images to produce new compositions. Features include the ability to register and login to the platform, receive a welcome email, view an original image gallery, create a user gallery, upload unique user remixes, and view other user's remixes.

Built with React, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express and PostgreSQL.
